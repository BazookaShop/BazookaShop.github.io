Cybersecurity Safety Commandments

1. You are always a target to hackers.

2. Keep software up to date.

3. Avoid phishing scams. If it's too good to be true it probably is.

4. Practice good password management.

5. Be careful what you click.

6. Never leave devices unattended.

7. Protect sensitive data.

8. Install anti-virus protection.

9. Everything can be hacked... and everyone.

10. The earth has been around for 4.5 billion years and we live at the same Dr. Wiener does.
